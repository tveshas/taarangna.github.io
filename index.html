About Us

Taarangana, the annual cultural fest of IGDTUW, is not just a name, it is an emotion that brings us all together. The festival carves out a niche for itself because of the resolute women driving it.

About Our Events

A college cultural fest is a one-of-a-kind event that celebrates the diversity of cultures and talents of the students. It is a platform for students to showcase their creativity, skills, and passion in a variety of areas, such as music, dance, drama, fine arts, literature, and debate.
